Abstract

यह शोध मानव-प्रजाति के भीतर सक्रिय दो मूल तंत्र— मन (Cognitive System) और हृदय (Experiential System)— के संतुलन का दार्शनिक एवं संरचनात्मक मॉडल प्रस्तुत करता है।

Dual System Model

Dimension Mind Heart
Time Past/Future Present
Base Memory Direct Experience
Goal Achievement Balance
Risk Ego & Conflict Passivity

Core Principles

  • Mind is a tool, not the master.
  • Heart is awareness, not emotion.
  • Science without sensitivity is dangerous.
  • Sensitivity without structure is incomplete.
  • Balance creates sustainable civilization.

Conclusion

Human capability brings responsibility. Balance between cognition and compassion can define the next phase of civilization.
